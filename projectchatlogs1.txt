Goal: Define core game loop, card system direction, and initial card pool.

Decisions Locked:
- Platform: Tabletop Simulator mod (Lua, TTS save workflow).
- Game structure: MTG-inspired card game; up to 6 players; starting LP 25.
- Draw rules: draw 5 on game start; draw 1 at turn start.
- Energy rules: energy carries over turn-to-turn; default cap 10 unless card overrides.
- Field limits per player: max 6 Unit slots + 6 Action slots.
- Play lock rules:
  - If 6 continuous action cards are in play, that player cannot play more action cards.
  - If 6 unit slots and 6 action slots are full, that player cannot play any cards.
- Deck construction: 40-50 cards total; default max copies per card is 1 unless card text allows more.
- Set mixing: cards from different animated shows can be mixed in one deck.
- Combat timing: attack phase at end of turn.
- Summoning timing: units cannot attack on summon unless they have SuperSpeed.
- Blocking: optional unless a card says must block.
- Instant-speed cards: lightning-symbol cards can be played during opponents' turns and always cost energy.
- Any-target rule: when a card says unit can attack any player, controlling player chooses the target.
- Continuous marker: infinity symbol is used for continuous action cards.
- Energy cards are not continuous.

- Large initial card batch drafted and revised card-by-card.
