Open Questions To Resolve During Item 1:
- None. Rules Spec v1 finalized in RULES_SPEC_V1.md.

Rules Locked During Item 1:
- Drawing from an empty deck causes immediate loss unless a card controlled by that player explicitly prevents or replaces that loss.
- Attackers can target units directly.
- Only units with the `target` attribute can be targeted directly for attacks.
- Multiple blockers may block the same attacker.
- A single blocker cannot block multiple attackers unless a card explicitly allows it.
- A player with a full board may still play Energy cards and activate legal card effects from hand.
- Liberation counts as a hand-activated effect for full-board legality purposes.
- Lightning timing model for v1 uses broad windows: after a card is played or effect resolves, before attacks are declared, after blockers are declared, and during end phase before cleanup.
- Simultaneous trigger ordering resolves in reverse play order (newest to oldest).
